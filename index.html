Summer

One of the best parts of summer is swimming.

Swimming in Indiana is almost only done in the summer, this is why it is one of the best parts of it. Here are some pros and cons to swimming during the summer.

Pros

  1. Cool down
  2. Have fun with freinds
  3. Play games
  4. You can do tricks into the water
  5. You can go cliff jumping

Cons

  1. Can be boring alone
  2. Could be expensie if you dont have a place to swim
  3. There is always a chance of drowning
